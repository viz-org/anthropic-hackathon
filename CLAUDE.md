# Super MCP World - Claude Code London Hack Night 03

**"Build an MCP App" Hack Night** | February 20, 2026 | 18:00-22:00 | London | Free

## What Is This?

A free hack night where you build a functional **MCP (Model Context Protocol) App** that solves an everyday workplace challenge. 90 minutes to hack, then pitch to the judges.

- **Format:** Solo or teams of 2 (max)
- **Submissions:** Open until Sunday Feb 22 at 23:59 (polish over the weekend!)
- **Food:** Pizza and beverages provided
- **Register:** [Luma](https://luma.com/ictqc1wp)
